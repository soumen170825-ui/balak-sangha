About Balak Sangha

Balak Sangha Football Club, established in 1954, is one of the oldest and most respected football clubs in Jagatdal Buri Bot Tala (743127). Since its foundation, the club has been committed to promoting football, youth development, and community unity. Over the decades, Balak Sangha has nurtured local talents and created a strong sporting culture in the area.

Our Goals

  • Promote grassroots football development
  • Encourage youth participation in sports
  • Build discipline, teamwork, and leadership among players
  • Represent Jagatdal in district and local tournaments
  • Develop future professional football players

Achievements

  • Successfully organizing local football tournaments for many years
  • Participation in various local and district football competitions

Future Planning

  • Organize annual football tournaments
  • Provide structured training for young players
  • Upgrade sports equipment and facilities
  • Collaborate with local coaches and trainers
  • Expand participation in district & state-level tournaments
  • Promote football awareness in the local community
  • Produce talented players representing local leagues
  • Maintain a strong football tradition since 1954
  • Active involvement in community sports and youth engagement

Our Vision

To build a strong football culture and create opportunities for young talents while carrying forward the proud legacy of Balak Sangha since 1954.
