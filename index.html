About Us

Balak Sangha Football Club, established in 1954, is one of the oldest and most respected football clubs in Jagatdal, Ward No. 35 (743127). Since its foundation, the club has been committed to promoting football, youth development, and community unity.

Our Goals

  • Promote grassroots football development
  • Encourage youth participation in sports
  • Build discipline, teamwork, and leadership
  • Represent Jagatdal in tournaments
  • Develop future professional players

Our Achievements

  • Successfully organizing local tournaments
  • Participation in district competitions
  • Strong football tradition since 1954
  • Community sports involvement

Future Planning

  • Organize annual tournaments
  • Training for young players
  • Upgrade equipment
  • District & state tournaments
  • Football awareness program

Our Vision

To build a strong football culture and create opportunities for young talents while carrying forward the proud legacy of Balak Sangha since 1954.
